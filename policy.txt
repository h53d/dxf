Privacy Policy for DXF Assistant
Effective Date: 2025-03-17

1. Introduction
DXF Assistant values your privacy and is committed to protecting your data. This Privacy Policy outlines how DXF Assistant manages your data, including collection, storage, and security practices. By using DXF Assistant, you agree to this policy.

2. Data Collection
DXF Assistant does not collect, share, or transmit any personal or usage data. All data processed within the app remains locally stored on your device.

3. Data Security
Your data security is our priority. DXF Assistant operates data offline, ensuring that all files and data are managed directly on your device, without external transmissions. Unless you enable sharing for this app or its data through Apple iCloud, all data is stored locally on your device.

4. Access to Data
The data you manage within DXF Assistant, such as files and drawings exports, is accessible only to you. DXF Assistant does not have any mechanism to remotely access, upload, or back up your data outside of your device.

5. Offline Functionality
DXF Assistant is designed to function offline, there is no need for Internet connection, nor any background or hidden data transfer processes. This enhances both security and privacy by keeping all information on the device.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this policy. Continued use of DXF Assistant after any modifications signifies acceptance of these changes.
